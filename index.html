Project background

The bicycle production industry in Europe is facing challenges, with a 5.26% decrease in bicycle sales profits from 2015 to 2016. This fact is regrettable because from 2014 to 2015, bicycle sales profits increased by 11.56%.

Project Goal

To increase the number of profits up to 5-11% in 1 year.

Project Scope: Data Set Bike Sales in Europe

In Europe bikes dataset have 113.000 data
In this dataset 18 columns;

  1. Date
  2. Day
  3. Month
  4. Year
  5. Customer_Age
  6. Age_Group
  7. Customer_Gender
  8. Country
  9. State
  10. Product_Category
  11. Sub_Category
  12. Product
  13. Order_Quantity
  14. Unit_Cost
  15. Unit_Price
  16. Profit
  17. Cost
  18. Revenue

Research Question

  • Will reducing costs increase profits from bicycle sales?
  • Will increasing bicycle sales increase sales profits?
  • Will the reduction in bicycle prices, which is expected to increase sales, increase sales profits?
