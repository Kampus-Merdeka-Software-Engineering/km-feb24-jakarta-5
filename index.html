Business Insight

Conclusion

To increase our profit up to 5-11%, we need to focus on specific countries, product categories, and customer age range that can make a significant contribution to increased profitability. Because when we focus on specific target, we can reduce the unit cost and gain more profit. We also should focus on our engagement to increase our profit.

Reccomendations
  • Identify the negative factors, the cause of the decreasing of bicycle sales profit. Try to reduce or fix the negative factors.
  • Paying attention to engagement strategies, such as: market trends, age target, and advertisement. Increasing the engagement with the right way and strategies can lead us to more customer.
  • Cost optimization, minimize wastage to optimize cost.
